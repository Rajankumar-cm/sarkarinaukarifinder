❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is GovtJobsFilter free to use?
Yes, completely free. No registration or payment is required to search and filter jobs.
How often are job listings updated?
We update listings regularly, typically as soon as new official notifications are released by the respective government organizations.
I found incorrect information in a job listing. What should I do?
Please use the contact form to report it. Include the job title and the error you noticed. We will review and correct it promptly.
Can I apply for jobs directly from this website?
We provide a direct link to the official government website for each job. Applications must be submitted on the official government portal only.
Is GovtJobsFilter affiliated with any government body?
No. We are an independent information portal. We are not affiliated with SSC, UPSC, RRB, or any other government organization.
