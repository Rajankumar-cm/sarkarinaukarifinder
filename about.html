About GovtJobsFilter

Helping Indian students find the right government job opportunity

🎯 Our Mission

GovtJobsFilter was created with one simple goal — to make it easier for every Indian student and job seeker to find government job opportunities that match their specific qualifications and age, without wasting time searching through dozens of websites.

Every year, millions of students in India miss important government job application deadlines simply because they did not know about the vacancy in time, or because they could not figure out whether they were eligible. We are here to solve exactly that problem.

📋 What We Cover

GovtJobsFilter covers government job notifications from across India, including:

Central Government: SSC (Staff Selection Commission), UPSC (Union Public Service Commission), Railway Recruitment Board (RRB/RRC), IBPS, SBI, and other central government departments.

State Government: State Public Service Commissions (PSC), State Police Recruitment, State Teaching Recruitment Boards, and other state-level government organizations across 28 states and 8 union territories.

Categories covered: Railway, SSC, UPSC, Banking, Police, Teaching, Defence/Army, Health/Medical, and State PSC jobs — covering qualifications from 10th Pass to Post Graduate level.

🔍 How Our Filter Works

Our smart filtering system works in three simple steps:

Step 1 — Enter Your Profile: Select your highest qualification, enter your current age, choose your state, and optionally pick a job category.

Step 2 — Instant Matching: Our system compares your profile against all currently active job notifications and shows only those where you meet the basic eligibility criteria.

Step 3 — Apply Directly: Each job listing shows the last date, number of vacancies, and a direct link to the official government website where you can apply.

Note: Always read the official notification carefully before applying. Eligibility criteria may include additional requirements such as physical standards, domicile requirements, or specific subject combinations which may vary by post.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

GovtJobsFilter is an independent information portal and is not affiliated with any government organization, including SSC, UPSC, Railway Recruitment Board, or any State Government body.

All job information displayed on this website is sourced from official government notifications and websites. We make every effort to ensure accuracy, but we strongly recommend candidates to verify all details from the official notification before applying.

Always check the official government website for the most up-to-date information, including any changes to vacancy numbers, eligibility criteria, exam dates, or application deadlines.

📌 For the most accurate and latest information, always refer to the official website of the recruiting organization before submitting your application.
